How I Work

My Design Process

A structured approach that balances creativity with rigorous methodology.

🔭
Step 01
Research
User interviews, competitive analysis, heuristic evaluations. No assumptions.
💡
Step 02
Ideate
Crazy 8s, affinity mapping, design sprints. Generate 20 ideas, kill 18.
✏️
Step 03
Design
Lo-fi → hi-fi, component-first thinking, design tokens from day one.
🧪
Step 04
Test
Moderated sessions, tree testing, 5-second tests. Data over opinions.
🔁
Step 05
Iterate
Ship, measure, improve. Design is never finished — only shipped.
